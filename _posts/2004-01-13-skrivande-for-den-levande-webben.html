På A List Apart ger Mark Bernstein, forskningschef på Eastgate Systems, tio värdefulla råd för ett bättre skrivande för den levande, bubblande och föränderliga webben. Följande utdrag fann jag själv väldigt inspirerande:

Write for a reason, and know why you write. Whether your daily updates concern your work life, your hobbies, or your innermost feelings, write passionately about things that matter.

If you are writing for the Living Web, you must write consistently. You need not write constantly, and you need not write long, but you must write often.

If you know your facts and have done your homework, you have a right to your opinion. State it clearly. Never waffle, whine, or weasel.
